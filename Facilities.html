Facilities

Library

The college has a well–stocked central library with a vast collection of books, journals, magazines, and digital resources to support academic and research activities.

Laboratories

State–of–the–art laboratories are available for all departments, equipped with modern instruments and software to provide practical exposure to students.

Computer Centre

High-speed internet connectivity and advanced computing facilities are provided to students to support learning and research.

Hostel

Separate hostel facilities for boys and girls with secure accommodation, hygienic food, and recreational amenities are available inside the campus.

Transport

The college operates a fleet of buses covering major routes to provide safe and convenient transportation for students and staff.

Sports & Gymnasium

Excellent sports facilities including playgrounds, indoor games, and a well–equipped gymnasium encourage physical fitness and teamwork.

Cafeteria

The campus cafeteria provides hygienic and affordable food options catering to diverse tastes of students.
