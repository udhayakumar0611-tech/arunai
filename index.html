Welcome to Arunai Engineering College

Arunai Engineering College is an autonomous institution dedicated to providing quality technical education. The college focuses on academic excellence, innovation, and holistic development of students.

About the Institution

Established with a vision to produce skilled and ethical engineers, Arunai Engineering College offers industry-oriented programs supported by experienced faculty and modern infrastructure.

Courses Offered

* B.E – Computer Science and Engineering

* B.E – Electronics and Communication Engineering

* B.E – Electrical and Electronics Engineering

* B.E – Mechanical Engineering

* B.Tech – Information Technology

* MBA & M.E Programmes

Why Choose Arunai?

* Autonomous Institution with flexible curriculum

* Strong placement and training support
